TrackMyBets lets you track and analyse your betting history in one place.

About

When I started experimenting with betting, I noticed that betting apps are very unclear about your betting history. It is clear that they don't want you to understand where you're winning or losing. So I started tracking in a spreadsheet. It worked, but editing on mobile was cumbersome. I looked at existing bet tracking apps and found two groups: the simple ones with a bad user experience, and the more capable ones that lock features behind subscriptions.

I couldn't find an open app that let me export/import my data, apply deep yet friendly filters, or see my gains and losses clearly. So I built TrackMyBets.

I'm releasing it in the hope it helps other people lose less by making them understand where they're winning or losing money. There are no subscriptions. You can try it by adding a few events and bets; if you like it, buy once to unlock unlimited events and bets, along with other features.

Features

  • Track events and bets

    Create events for each match or session, add bets with stake, odds, and outcome. P/L is calculated automatically per event.

  • Manage multiple bankrolls

    Separate your money into different pools: by strategy, sport, or anything else. Track deposits, withdrawals, and balances independently.

  • Filter and analyze

    Combine date ranges, tags, and markets to slice your history. See ROI, win rate, biggest wins and losses, and a progression chart.

  • Organize with tags

    Tag events by sport, league, strategy, or source. Answer questions like "Which league performs best?" or "In which markets have I performed better this month?".

  • Export and import

    Back up your data as CSV files. Import works as a full restore.

  • Works the way you prefer

    Choose your currency, odds format (Decimal, Fractional, American), and date/time display.

What it doesn't do

  • It doesn't offer predictions or tips

    No social features, gambling products, or encouragement to bet more.

  • It doesn't store data in the cloud

    Everything stays on your device. You are responsible for backing up your data regularly.

  • It doesn't connect to bookmakers

    No live odds or external integrations.

Availability

TrackMyBets is free to download on Google Play. A Pro upgrade unlocks unlimited events/bets, CSV export/import, and multiple bankrolls.
