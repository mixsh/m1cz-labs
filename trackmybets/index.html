TrackMyBets lets you log, track, and analyse your betting history in one place — so you always know where you stand.

About

A personal project built to bring clarity to sports betting. Record every bet, review your performance over time, and spot the patterns that matter.

TrackMyBets is a record-keeping and analysis tool. It doesn't sell bets, offer tips, or encourage you to wager more. It's built to help you understand your betting, and not something that profits from it.

Features

  • Track events and bets

    Create events for each match or session, add bets with stake, odds, and outcome. P/L calculated automatically.

  • Manage multiple bankrolls

    Separate your money into different pools — by strategy, sport, or anything else. Track deposits, withdrawals, and balances independently.

  • Filter and analyze

    Combine date ranges, tags, and market selections to slice your history. See ROI, win rate, biggest wins and losses, and a progression chart.

  • Organize with tags

    Tag events by sport, league, strategy, or source. Answer questions like "Which league performs best?"

  • Custom market selections

    Define your own betting positions and reuse them across events. The app doesn't ship with sports or leagues — you build the structure that fits how you bet.

  • Export and import

    Back up your data as CSV files. Import works as a full restore. (Pro feature.)

  • Works the way you prefer

    Choose your currency, odds format (Decimal, Fractional, American), and date/time display.

What it doesn't do

  • It doesn't connect to bookmakers

    No live odds, no fetch requests, no external integrations.

  • It doesn't offer predictions or tips

    No social features, no gambling products, no encouragement to bet more.

  • It doesn't store data in the cloud

    Everything stays on your device. You are responsible for backing up your data.

Availability

TrackMyBets is free to download on Google Play. A Pro upgrade unlocks CSV export/import, unlimited bets, and multiple bankrolls.
